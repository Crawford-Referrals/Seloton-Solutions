Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a fee for using your referral service?

No, our loan referral service is completely free for borrowers. We receive compensation from our lending partners when we successfully match you with a loan product that meets your needs.

How quickly can I get approved for a loan?

The approval process varies by lender and loan type. Some lenders offer same-day approval decisions, while others may take 1-3 business days to review your application.

What credit score do I need to qualify?

We work with lenders who serve borrowers across the credit spectrum. While a higher score typically results in better rates, we have partners who specialize in working with borrowers who have less-than-perfect credit.

How much can I borrow?

Our lending partners offer personal loans ranging from $1,000 to $50,000, depending on your qualifications, income, and the purpose of the loan.
